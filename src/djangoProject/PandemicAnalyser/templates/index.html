PANDEMIC ANALYSER

This page shows different statistics gathered from the results of the sentiment analysis of a publicly available Twitter dataset.

The Daily page shows daily sentiment calculated from March 2020 to December 2022.

The Key Dates page shows the sentiment at key dates and compares it to the sentiment the week, month, and year of the key date.

The Models page shows the accuracy of all the models that were tested for sentiment analysis.

The About page shows information about the project and website.
